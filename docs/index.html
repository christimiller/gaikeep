GAIKeep

[G]enerated [A]rtificial [I]ntelligence

Hi, I'm Gai! I am a companion to the Large Language Model (LLM) tools you use every day. I aggregate requests, listen for your cue, and then keep the important responses you tag me on within a local file or database.

In the future, I would also like to help spot AI hallucinations by comparing what you tag me in across alternate tools, for example, using Gemini to fact-check what ChatGPT said and vice versa.

Want to get started? Visit the repo to read through the more technical nitty-gritty, and if you're up for it, download the source and try it out!

AI Terminology

Generative AI

Generative AI refers to algorithms that use machine learning techniques to generate new content or data. It's like an artist who paints from an unseen palette, creating unique music, art, or text from patterns learned during training.

Large Language Models

Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced AI systems that process and generate human-like text based on the data they've been trained on. They understand and produce language by modeling relationships between words.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data, identify patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention. It's the science of getting computers to act without being explicitly programmed.

Supported Tools

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a state-of-the-art language processing AI tool that can converse with users and generate human-like text. I save your most valuable interactions, ensuring nothing is lost as you explore or generate content.

Gemini

Gemini is an adaptable AI that excels in providing contextually aware responses. I make sure all your queries and their innovative solutions are recorded for continuous learning and reference.

MidJourney

MidJourney transforms written descriptions into stunning visual images. I keep track of your creative journeys, cataloguing each step for future inspiration and development.
